## Overview

The **Federal Signal Analytics Platform** ingests real-time open data across key United States federal agencies and public scientific repositories.

### Key Data Ingestion Sources

1. **SAM.gov Contract Opportunities**: Live federal contracting solicitations and procurement notices.
2. **openFDA Surveillance**: Drug & medical device adverse events, approvals, and recall notices.
3. **ClinicalTrials.gov**: Live clinical trial registrations and study status updates.
4. **USASpending.gov**: Federal award data and government spending metrics.
5. **arXiv Repository**: Early pre-print scientific papers in AI, biotech, quantum computing, and engineering.
6. **SEC EDGAR**: Corporate financial filings and material disclosure reports.
7. **USPTO**: Public patent data and innovation filings.
